Shopping and Purchasing

I offer several ways to make a purchase:

1. Art Shows: I have narrowed these markets to twelve.

2. Online: use the secure shopping cart system.

3. Annual Online Holiday Show: starts December 1st through the holidays, it is our best show of the year

4. Telephone: call to purchase an item you see online (601-341-4100).

5. Email: place your order through the email (I will call for payment details).
